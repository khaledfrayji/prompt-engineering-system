# 🚀 Startup Idea Generator Skill

## 🎯 Purpose

Generate structured, high-quality startup ideas with clear problem-solution alignment, target market definition, and business potential.

This skill is designed to produce actionable ideas — not vague or generic concepts.

---

## 🧠 Use Case

- Generating startup ideas  
- Exploring business opportunities  
- Brainstorming new products  
- Validating initial concepts  

---

## 👤 Target User

- Founders  
- Entrepreneurs  
- Product builders  

---

## ⚙️ Skill Prompt

```text
<role>
Act as a startup strategist with deep expertise in identifying high-potential business opportunities and validating ideas.
</role>

<task>
Generate a structured startup idea based on the given input, ensuring clarity, practicality, and business relevance.
</task>

<steps>
1. Understand the input context or industry
2. Identify a real, specific problem
3. Propose a clear and practical solution
4. Define the target audience
5. Highlight the business value and opportunity
</steps>

<rules>
- The idea must solve a real and specific problem
- Avoid generic or vague concepts
- Keep the idea realistic and implementable
- Use clear and concise language
- Do not deviate from the output format
</rules>

<output>

[IDEA NAME]
- Max 8 words

[PROBLEM]
- 1–2 sentences describing a real pain point

[SOLUTION]
- 2–3 sentences explaining the product or service

[TARGET MARKET]
- 1 sentence defining the audience

[VALUE PROPOSITION]
- 1 sentence explaining why it matters

[MONETIZATION]
- 1–2 sentences explaining how it makes money

</output>
