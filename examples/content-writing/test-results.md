### ✅ Test 1 — Standard Input

**Input:**  
AI will replace junior developers  

**Output:**

[TITLE]  
Will AI Replace Junior Developers?

[HOOK]  
AI is changing coding faster than anyone expected.

[CONTENT]  
AI tools are increasingly capable of generating code, debugging, and even explaining logic. This raises concerns about whether junior developers will still have a place in the industry. However, the reality is more nuanced than simple replacement.

Instead of removing opportunities, AI is shifting the role of junior developers toward higher-level thinking. Learning how to use AI effectively is becoming more important than memorizing syntax or writing boilerplate code.

[KEY POINTS]

### ✅ Test 2 — Broad Topic

**Input:**  
Why startups fail early  

**Output:**

[TITLE]  
Why Startups Fail Early

[HOOK]  
Most startups don’t fail from lack of ideas.

[CONTENT]  
Early-stage startups often collapse due to poor execution rather than weak concepts. Founders may build products without validating demand, leading to solutions that nobody actually needs. This disconnect between product and market is one of the most common failure points.

Additionally, teams often move too fast without clear priorities. Resources get wasted, focus gets lost, and momentum fades. Without structured decision-making, even strong ideas struggle to survive.

[KEY POINTS]  
- Building without validation leads to zero real demand  
- Poor prioritization wastes time and critical resources  
- Lack of focus slows down execution and growth  

[CONCLUSION]  
Startups fail when execution doesn’t match ambition.

### ⚠️ Test 4 — Niche Topic

**Input:**  
Benefits of edge computing in IoT  

**Output:**

[TITLE]  
Edge Computing in IoT

[HOOK]  
Processing data closer changes everything in IoT systems.

[CONTENT]  
Edge computing allows IoT devices to process data locally instead of relying entirely on centralized cloud systems. This reduces latency and enables faster decision-making, which is critical for real-time applications like autonomous systems or industrial automation.

By minimizing data transfer, edge computing also reduces bandwidth usage and improves system efficiency. This leads to more scalable and responsive IoT architectures.

[KEY POINTS]  
- Local processing reduces latency in real-time IoT systems  
- Lower bandwidth usage improves scalability and efficiency  
- Faster decisions enable critical real-time applications  

[CONCLUSION]  
Edge computing unlocks real-time intelligence in IoT.
